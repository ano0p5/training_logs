Benefits for everyone

Dirk van den Broek is the best supermarket in the Netherlands. With us you can do your daily shopping for a low price. This is because we keep a close eye on our own costs - we call this our Price Principles . For example, we do not have a delivery service, no special savings campaigns and no customer cards. This allows us to keep costs low and you can shop for groceries cheaply. Choosing Dirk means effortlessly saving on groceries. Look in our Dirk brochure now for the latest offers or visit a supermarket in your area .

  • Always the best offer per piece
  • Without subscription or customer card
